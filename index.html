About

Background & Story

I'm a Lead Software Engineer with over 11 years of experience building products that real people use every day — from e-commerce apps serving millions of shoppers across the Middle East, to digital onboarding platforms that brought banking to the field.

My career spans the full arc of frontend evolution: I started with native Android, moved through Cordova hybrid apps, built cross-platform experiences with React Native, and for the past 4+ years have focused on React.js enterprise web applications. I understand platforms at a deep level because I've built for each of them.

Currently at Landmark Group, I lead the modernisation of a large-scale Retail Management System — migrating legacy Oracle Forms modules into scalable, performant React web applications. I work closely with business analysts, backend teams, and UX to transform complex enterprise workflows into intuitive interfaces.

I embrace AI-assisted development (Claude, Cursor AI, GPT-4) as a force multiplier, and believe the best engineers combine technical depth with strong product thinking.
